WHAT IS TERRAHACKS?


TerraHacks is a virtual ideathon hosted by students where both new and experienced students can work together on ideas, learn valuable brainstorming skills, and have a wonderful time pitching their ideas. Our events are backed by HackClub, a 501 (c)(3) public charity registered in CA. All donations are tax - deductible. Please feel free to email us at terrahackscomp@gmail.com if you have any questions or concerns.

WHAT IS AN IDEATHON?

An ideathon is a competition where students present their ideas to solve some of the challenges and problems our world faces. In our event, students will choose to "answer" one of the given prompts. Participants collaborate and work in teams to ideate and design innovative solutions to these problems and present their solution with a video/powerpoint presentation.

DO I NEED ANY EXPERIENCE IN IDEATHONS?

No! In fact, TerraHacks is a great way to begin your ideathon experience. While it is helpful to have some prior knowledge on general topics, we will have resources to help you develop your idea.

WHAT WILL I NEED?

You will need a computer to work on and submit your submission.

WHO CAN ATTEND?

This event is open to students who will be high schoolers in the 2021-2022 school year ONLY. No middle schoolers will be allowed. College students may sign up to judge the submissions. Email terrahackscomp@gmail.com if you have any questions or concerns.

WHEN IS THE EVENT?

The next event will be starting on August 17, 2021 and will run until September 6, 2021. Each teach can start brainstorming and working on their ideas when the competition begins and can submit their idea (as a video or a document) until the competition ends.

TEAM REQUIREMENTS?

You need at least 1 person per team and 3 people at most.

HOW DO I SUBMIT MY IDEA?

Submissions will be accepted in a video or document format. For the video, we will accept a video no longer than 3 minutes quickly describing the idea. For the document, you are limited to 1 page describing your idea.
